What This Tests

The LOOI follow feature needs the front camera to stay alive for Roboflow. The back probe only works if the browser can open a second camera without stopping or muting the first stream.

Pass means both previews keep moving and both video tracks report live. Fail means the second camera request errors, or opening it ends/mutes the first stream.
